chole bhature recipe

Description

chole bhature is famous indian dish, it is comprised of fluffy bread and chicpead curry.

It is served with a chilled glass of lassi which is basically sweet curd stired for very long hours, it tastes heavenly with chole bhature

ingredients

  • chickpea
  • masale
  • wheat flour
  • oil

steps to create the dish

  1. take oil into a big kadhai
  2. midium flame let oil heat up
  3. put boiled chicpeas into it with masale's
  4. now lets create bhature which is bread by making a dough
  5. make the bread in oil too
  6. the dish is ready

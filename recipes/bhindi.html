bhindi recipe

Description

bhindi is a popular indian vegetable dish made from fresh okra cooked with spices. it is simple, healthy and very tasty.

It is usually served hot with roti or rice and is a common dish in many indian households.

ingredients

  • fresh bhindi (okra)
  • oil
  • onion
  • tomato
  • turmeric powder
  • red chili powder
  • salt

steps to create the dish

  1. wash and dry the bhindi properly
  2. cut the bhindi into small pieces
  3. heat oil in a pan on medium flame
  4. add chopped onions and cook until golden brown
  5. add tomatoes and cook until soft
  6. add spices and mix well
  7. add bhindi and cook until soft and slightly crispy
  8. the dish is ready to serve hot
